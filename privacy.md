# Letteracy — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 24, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Letteracy ("the App", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

- **Account information**: Email address, display name, username (handle), and password.
- **Profile information**: Biography, avatar image, and reading interests/genres.
- **Content you create**: Posts, blog entries, comments, club messages, and discussion replies.
- **Reading activity**: Books added to your reading journal, wishlist items, and collection preferences.
- **Social connections**: Users you follow and users you have blocked.
- **Images**: Avatar photos and images attached to posts or blog entries.

### Information Collected Automatically

- Session authentication tokens necessary for the App to function and keep you signed in.

### Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect precise or approximate location data.
- We do not access your device contacts, phone number, or call history.
- We do not use cookies, third-party analytics, or advertising trackers.
- We do not collect financial or payment information. The App does not process any transactions.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the App's core features (feed, clubs, writing studio, book discovery, reading journal).
- Display your profile and content to other users within the App.
- Authenticate your identity and maintain your session.
- Enable social features such as following, liking, commenting, and club participation.
- Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines (including processing reports and enforcing blocks).
- Respond to support requests or privacy inquiries.

We **do not** sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored using **Supabase**, a cloud database platform with servers located in the United States.
- Passwords are handled by Supabase Auth using industry-standard hashing algorithms and are never stored in plain text.
- Images (avatars and post attachments) are stored in Supabase Storage with row-level security policies restricting access.
- All data transmission between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.
- We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services strictly for content display and reference purposes:

- **Google Books API** — to display book metadata and cover images in the discovery feature.
- **Wikipedia API** — to display literary reference articles in the Index feature.
- **Gutendex API** — to display public domain book information.

These services may receive search queries you enter within the App. We do not share your account information or personal data with these services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third party except in the following limited circumstances:

- When required by law, regulation, subpoena, or legal process.
- To protect the rights, safety, or property of Letteracy, its users, or the public.
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case you would be notified.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data through your profile in the App at any time.
- **Edit** your profile information (name, handle, bio, avatar, interests) at any time.
- **Delete your content** (posts, blog entries, comments) at any time through the App.
- **Delete your account** permanently through the App's profile settings. This removes all your personal data, content, social connections, and stored files from our servers.
- **Block** other users to prevent their content from appearing in your feeds and to prevent interaction.
- **Report** content or users that violate our Community Guidelines.

If you wish to exercise any additional rights regarding your personal data, or if you have questions about your data, please contact us at the email provided below.

## 7. Data Retention

- Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.
- When you delete individual content (a post, comment, or blog entry), it is removed from our servers promptly.
- When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted, including: your profile, all posts, blog entries, comments, club messages, club memberships, reading journal entries, wishlist items, collections, social connections, blocked users list, reports you submitted, and all stored image files.
- We do not retain copies or backups of deleted account data.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Letteracy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has created an account or provided us with personal information, we will take prompt steps to delete that account and information. If you believe a child under 13 is using the App, please contact us immediately.

## 9. International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your data is transferred to and stored on servers in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer and acknowledge that data protection laws in the United States may differ from those in your country of residence.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the latest revision at the top of this page. If we make material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users within the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, your personal data, or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at madeforwebsiteneeds@gmail.com .
